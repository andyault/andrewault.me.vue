Game With No Title is a space-themed twin-stick dungeon crawler currently under development. The game features Man With No Name (MWNN), as astronaut who has just left his home planet, Probably Not Earth (PNE). He'll probably use one of these storylines:

  • MWNN's significant other has been kidnapped by space aliens or pirates or something, and he's going throughout his solar system to save her
  • MWNN works for NASA/etc, and he has been sent on a mission to study/collect samples from the sun. PNE would probably be on the edge of the solar system, and MWNN would go from planet to planet to get to the sun, then from planet to planet to get back. Might use a time limit or something?
  • Suggestions welcome! (I'm bad at writing)

Gameplay concepts:

  • Each planet will represent a "floor," will be procedurally generated, and will ideally feature unique enemies
  • The player will travel between planets via shuttle, and must gather fuel on each planet to continue
  • Enemies will use aggro, and will attack the player within a certain distance of their starting point
  • The player will attack via some sort of gun/projectile weapon

Possible concepts:

  • Different weapon types (possibly borderlands-style weapons?)
  • Shuttle fuel is collected all at once, and protected by a boss
  • Shuttle fuel is collected by exploring each planet until enough is collected (could need to be refined, and a boss could attack while refining?)
